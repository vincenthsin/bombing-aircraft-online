# Bombing Aircraft Online - Game Rules

## Objective
The goal of **Bombing Aircraft Online** is to destroy the enemy's air fleet before they destroy yours.
It is a turn-based strategy game played on a 10x10 grid.

## The Fleet
Each player commands a squadron of **3 Aircraft**.
Every aircraft has a specific shape occupying **10 grid cells**.

### Aircraft Shape
The aircraft is a **Heavy Bomber** (10 Cells):
```
      H        (Head)
    WWBWW      (Neck/Wings)
      B        (Body)
     TTT       (Tail)
```

## Gameplay Flow
### 1. Preparation Phase (Placement)
- **Grid**: A 10x10 battlefield.
- **Action**: Place your 3 aircraft.
- **Deployment**: Click "DEPLOY SQUADRON".

### 2. Battle Phase (Combat)
- **Victory**: Destroy all enemy aircraft (30 hits total).
